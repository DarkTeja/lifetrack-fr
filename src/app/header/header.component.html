🚀 How it Works

LifeTrack AI aggregates your daily habits across Finance, Health, Work, and Learning into a unified Holistic Life Score. The more consistent you are, the higher your score climbs!

📖 Quick Start Guide

  • 1. Navigate: Use the sidebar (left on desktop, bottom on mobile) to switch between modules.
  • 2. Log Daily: In each module, use the "Add" buttons to log your expenses, workouts, or study hours.
  • 3. Set Targets: Go to Finance to set monthly budgets, or Study to set learning goals.
  • 4. AI Sync: Click "Deep Analysis" on the Dashboard to let the AI analyze your trends and give you tips.
